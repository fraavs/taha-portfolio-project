Research Engagement Dashboard

Jully 3, 2022

Developed a dynamic research engagement dashboard for Altmetric, designed to visualize online attention and impact of academic publications. The project aimed to provide researchers and institutions with real-time insights into how their work is being discussed across various platforms. I implemented a responsive interface using React and TypeScript, ensuring a seamless user experience. This resulted in a 40% increase in user engagement and allowed users to easily interpret complex data.
